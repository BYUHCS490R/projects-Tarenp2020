Tempura

Ingredients

Shrip

  • 10 prawns
  • *As Needed* Cooking Oil
  • *As Needed* Corn Starch

Batter

  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 cup All-Purpose Flower
  • 4 Tbsp Vodka

Dipping Sauce

  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp Hondashi
  • 3 Tbsp Soy Scauce
  • 3 Tbsp Mirin
  • 2 tsp Granulated Sugar

How to Cook

In a saucepan, over medium heat add all the sauce ingrediants together. Once the sugar is desolved its done.

Next you'll prep the prawns by removing the outer shell, but keeping the tail. Don't forget to remove the big vein on the back.

To finish up the prawn prep, dust the prawns in Cornstarch.

Mix all of the batter ingrediants together, making sure the batter stays cold and not overmixing, its okay to have lumps.

heat up Cooking Oil in a pan, about 2 inches worth. The oil needs to be 340-350 F and stay in that range the whole time.

You can then take the preped prawns and dip them in the batter and place then in the hot oil for about a minuet.

Once its cooked place the prawn on a wire rack as you do the next prawn.

When you finish cooking you can then dip them in the sauce and eat!
