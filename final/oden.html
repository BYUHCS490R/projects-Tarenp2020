Oden

Oden is a soup based dish that's ment to warm you up on a cold day!

To start you can make the broth. Typically you put:

  • 6 cups Dashi (a japanese stock made from kombu and katsuobush)
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 2 Tbsp mirin
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt

and in to a pot and bring it to a boil.

After the base is made it's up to you to decide what ingrediants to use. It's commonly one of each of the following catagories are present in an Oden, with even multiple from one catigory being present.
