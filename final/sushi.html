Sushi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Sushi Rice
  • 3 Cups Cold Water
  • 1/4 cup Rice Vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 pack Unseasoned Nori
  • Sliced Vegetables
  • Sliced Seafood
  • Pickeled Ginger
  • Wasabi

How to Cook

Cook rice

Next you'll mix the salit, sugar, and rice vinegar together.

mic cooled rice with the rice vinegar mixture.

Cut up your seafood into its long thin slices as well as the vegetables.

Now we are ready to turn them into a sushi roll, place the Nori on a bamboo mat. You lay a thin layer of rice on it and then place a line of the seafood and vegetable

You can then use the bamboo mat to help roll it all up and you'll be left with a long tube.

Finally slice the tube into cicles and top them with pickeled ginger and wasabi as desierd.
