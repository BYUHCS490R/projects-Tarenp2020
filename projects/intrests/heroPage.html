I am picking to write about a comic artist that I have liked for a while who goes by Picolo on Instagram. I have a couple reasons why I love his art. I think that he is amazing at drawing everyday moments and making them seem so special and fun. He captures the relationship between his characters well. I also love his coloring style. He is able to use muted tones to then get specifics to pop, which I just love. I feel like he can capture the magic of any moment in your life, even something as simple as putting on your shoes.

Teen Titans
He is always drawing characters from a popular cartoon called Teen Titans. They are 5 super heros that team up with the head being Robin, Batmans sidekick. However, Batman is not in the show.
