Taren's Art Rant

I have always loved to draw and create all kinds of art. Typically I will sketch but when I was younger I had lots of experiences and opportunities to take potery classes so I also love to work with clay to create bowls, tea pots, and other silly nicknacks.

I am not a profesional artist by all means so I get embaressed sharing my art with people. I do it for fun and I am still trying to create an art style I like. My favorite thing to draw is faces. I have always loved to draw them. It's so satisfity to get a faace right. I hope one day to be able to satisfyingly draw my future family's portrait.
