Gyudon Beef Bowl

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Medium Onions
  • 1lb thinkly sliced beef
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 mirin
  • 2 soy sauce
  • 1 Dahsi Stock
  • 1 Scallion
  • 2 tsp Toasted Sesame Seeds

How to Cook

Heat up vegetable oil in a skillet and cook the sliced onions

After add the beef and sugar until the beef is brown. Then add mirin, soy sauce, and dashi.

Simmer for about 10 minutes until the stock is a thin sauce.

Cook the eggs sunny side up on another pan. Once the meat is done you'll put it on top of a bowl of rice and top it off with the egg., scallions and toasted sesame seeds!
