Okonomiyaki

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1/4 Cabbage
  • 2/3 Plain Flour
  • 2 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1/2 Warm Water
  • 1 tsp Dashi Powder
  • 3 Eggs
  • 4-3 Pices of Bacon
  • 1-2 Green Onion
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Topping

  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Okonomiyaki Sauce
  • Seaweed Flakes

How to Cook

Start with mixing all the batter ingrediants in a bowl

After its all mixed then you will pour the ingrdeants out onto a griddle, like a pancake! You can also lay out the bacon after this step for better posisioning if you want.

Again just like a pancake once it seems cooked on one side and not liquidy, you flip it over to fry the other side.

After the Okonomiyaki is fully cooked you can place it on a plate and add as much toppings as you would like!
